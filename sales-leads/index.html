What is Lightning Leads?

Lightning Leads is an all-in-one lead generation software and CRM platform. We generate in-market sales leads and provide you with all the tools necessary to sell that customer. All of your leads, texts, calls, emails, deals, and files are available in one place, accessible with a single login.

Do you have a free trial?

No, we don’t offer any version of our services for free. All of our services are custom, and they require a lot of human effort to activate and manage. However, we do offer free DEMOs so you can make sure we are a good fit for your needs.

Is Lightning Leads available in Canada?

Lightning Leads services are currently available in the United States only. We are actively working on releasing a Canadian service as soon as possible. Please continue to monitor our pricing page for updates on the Canadian service launch date.

What are your Car Buyer leads?

Car buyer leads are people who are actively shopping to purchase a vehicle in your local market. We will prescreen every lead, asking them what car they want to buy, how much money they have to put down, and what monthly payment they can afford. Car buyer leads are generated through a wide range of online marketing with a primary goal of capturing hot shoppers looking to buy.

What are your Car Seller leads?

Car seller leads are people who are in the market looking to sell, trade, upgrade, or downgrade whatever they are driving right now. We find all the local for-sale-by-owner listings and send out a semiautomatic call to action asking the seller if they would be interested in selling or trading in their vehicle to the dealership. Once they reply, you will take it from there.

Are my leads shared with other dealers?

No, we do not send the same lead to multiple dealers. Every account gets its own feeds, and every time a lead is generated, the customer will receive your contact details informing them to whom we sent their request.

Who can sign up for your service?

We work with individual salespeople and entire dealer groups. So you can sign up yourself for an individual 1 user account or sign up an entire dealership with multiple users. We deliver exactly the same level of service and technology regardless if you sign up as a business or an individual.

How do I sign up for an account?

You can sign up on our website at https://Lightning Leads.com/sales-leads/new_account.php 24/7; no need to call or email. After we receive your application and invoice payment, it can take us 72+ hours to activate your account. Once your account is activated, one of our account managers will contact you with your login information and schedule a live training call so we can show you around the software.

Can I make changes after I sign up?

Yes, you can make changes or update any aspect of your account once you get your login. You have full control to update your service plan, website, templates, files, and all other CRM settings on your own without a need to contact us. We find all the local for-sale-by-owner listings and send out a semiautomatic call to action asking the seller if they would be interested in selling or trading in their vehicle to the dealership. Once they reply, you will take it from there.

Are my leads shared with other dealers?

No, we do not send the same lead to multiple dealers. Every account gets its own feeds, and every time a lead is generated, the customer will receive your contact details informing them to whom we sent their request.

Who can sign up for your service?

We work with individual salespeople and entire dealer groups. So you can sign up yourself for an individual 1 user account or sign up an entire dealership with multiple users. We deliver exactly the same level of service and technology regardless if you sign up as a business or an individual.

How do I sign up for an account?

You can sign up on our website at https://Lightning Leads.com/sales-leads/new_account.php 24/7; no need to call or email. After we receive your application and invoice payment, it can take us 72+ hours to activate your account. Once your account is activated, one of our account managers will contact you with your login information and schedule a live training call so we can show you around the software.

Can I make changes after I sign up?

Yes, you can make changes or update any aspect of your account once you get your login. You have full control to update your service plan, website, templates, files, and all other CRM settings on your own without a need to contact us. However, if you ever need help, your dedicated account manager will be just a phone call away.
